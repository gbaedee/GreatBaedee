About Me

My name is Great Leh Baedee and I’m currently a junior at Texas Southern University. I’m study Computer Science and computer Engineering Technology. I have teaching kids from middle to high school how to create Android Applications since December 2014, Aside from that I was the Vice- President and Secretary of Cyber Internet Society Student Association (CISSA); which we introduce and enhance our knowledge of cyber security practices as well as the Kali Linux Operating System environment. Additionally, this helps me expose myself to real world penetration testing scenarios through testing a mock network, establishing honeypots, and exposing users to the use of Virtual Machines. Right now I’m the secretary of Texas Southern University’s Mobile Application Development Group (TSU MAD) in which we introduce and enhance our software development capability in the mobile application environment that will promote entrepreneurship. We expose ourselves to real world software development process through actual software product development, which sharpened my programming skills on real world problems.
